    • Business Description

    Sissy.co is a new sole proprietorship business covering the area around Seri Manjung, Perak. Cakesicles, Korean Tom and Jerry cake, and Pinata cake will be offered with a variety of flavors, sizes, styles, toppings, and decorations. The business is owned by Nur Fatini Aqilah as a general manager, the administration manager is Nur Aqilah, the marketing manager is Nurul Ain, the production/operation manager is Nursyamimi Syadni, and the financial manager is Ainin Adha. The total of other workers is four workers. The date the business was started is on 11 January 2021. The idea of a company name comes from the people that always called the founder a ‘Sis’. Then, she was decided to name ‘Sissy.co’ as her business name. The tagline used is ‘something sweet inside’. What began as a hobby for us quickly turned into a prospective business venture. Sissy.co will also include a discount to the customer that buy large orders for big events. We provide new type of dessert that is healthier than other desserts sold at a market. The customers will buy our products because it may seem interesting for the consumers since it is new in their eyes and taste buds. They need to order at least 3 days before purchasing to ensure enough time for preparation. The uniqueness of our business from the other competitors is, we provide reward loyalty to existing customers. Additionally, the age range of our customers consists of 70% are teenagers and the other 30% are 30 years old and above.
